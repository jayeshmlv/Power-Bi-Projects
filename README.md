# Mobile Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze mobile phone sales across different cities, brands, customer ratings, payment methods, and monthly trends. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor business performance and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Objective
- Analyze sales performance across regions and brands.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Generate actionable business insights.

---

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Sales, Quantity, Transactions, Average Price)
- Sales by City
- Brand-wise Performance
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Customer Ratings
- Payment Method Analysis
- Top Selling Models
- Interactive Filters

---

## Key Insights
- Generated **769M** in total sales from **19K** units sold.
- Most customers gave **5-star ratings**, indicating high customer satisfaction.
- Apple generated the highest revenue among all brands.
- Sales peaked during **March, July, and October**.
- Customers used all payment methods almost equally.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Increase marketing in low-performing cities.
- Maintain inventory before peak sales months.
- Promote best-selling models during festive seasons.
- Offer EMI and cashback options to boost premium smartphone sales.
